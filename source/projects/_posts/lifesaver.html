Lifesaver Clock

"Did you know your clock turned into a lifesaver? You should fix that."

So this one wasn't very creative. There was a post on the Make blog about putting a clock in a circular tin so I decided to do it. I went out and bought some lifesavers and a clock, then ate the lifesavers and gutted the clock. The only real work I had to do was drill a hole in the top of the tin and shave off the corners of the clock a little bit to slip it inside.
